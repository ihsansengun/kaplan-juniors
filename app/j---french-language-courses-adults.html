French courses for students aged 16+ with Alpadia from Kaplan

OUR FRENCH COURSES FOR ADULTS

Learn French in France or Switzerland with Alpadia Language Schools, part of the Kaplan family

  • Prepare for an official French exam: DELF/DALF or TCF

  • 20 years of experience in providing language education to students from all over the world

  • Full immersion experience including an exciting weekly social program

  • Swiss quality standards in all our schools ensure that students get the most out of their learning experience

Our French courses are provided by Alpadia Language Schools, a leading education company with over 20 years of experience in providing Swiss-quality language tuition.

CHOOSE YOUR FRENCH COURSE

We provide French courses to suit students of all levels, from one-week intensives for beginners to longer academic programs for entry into universities in France and Switzerland. We are an official center for the TCF exam and are close to regional DELF/DALF exam locations.
